# Faceless EdTech Strategy

> The company picture as it stands at end of April 29, 2026. Captures decisions made in the Mask design conversation.

---

## What Faceless is now

A multi-product EdTech and creator network company built around a single thesis: **modern earning skills for students, taught through real-world campaigns and AI-assisted education.**

Four products that reinforce each other:

| Product | Status | What it is |
|---|---|---|
| **Faceless Hub** | Live (in development) | Campus creator network — students earn from brand campaigns |
| **Mask** | Live; in polish (Phase 3.1) | AI co-host that runs alongside Baz in college sessions |
| **Faceless Academy** | Concept stage | Free content + paid cohorts — courses sold online |
| **Mask Licensing** | Future product | Sell Mask + syllabus + playbook to other colleges and educators |

---

## The company-level thesis

The combination is what makes this defensible:

- **Real distribution** — 1,500+ students onboarded, brand partners, daily content
- **Real proof** — students earning ₹5K-50K through Hub campaigns (testimonials)
- **Real teaching** — Mask + curriculum is genuinely better than what most colleges deliver

Most EdTech has one of these. Faceless will have all three.

---

## The flywheel

```
Hub students → become Academy testimonials
Academy students → become Hub creators
Both → fuel content for marketing
Both → prove the model that licenses to other colleges
Mask → connective tissue, scales it all
Sessions → feed every product simultaneously
```

Every action in one product strengthens the others. This is the structural advantage.

## Business model: free + paid + licensing

### Free content (top of funnel)
- Clipped Mask sessions on YouTube/Instagram/X
- Free intro track on the website
- Build-in-public content from Baz

### Paid cohorts (middle of funnel — primary revenue)
- 4-week cohort-based courses
- Live access to Mask sessions + community + GRD alumni TAs
- ₹4,999 - ₹9,999 per cohort
- Earning eligibility through Hub campaigns

### Mask licensing (bottom of funnel — high margin)
- Sold to other colleges, bootcamps, educators
- Includes Mask instance, full syllabus, asset library, playbook
- Tiered pricing:
  - College club: ₹50K - ₹1L per year
  - Coding bootcamp / private institute: ₹2 - ₹5L per year
  - Individual creator/teacher: ₹15K - ₹25K per year

## Operating model: capture + prepare in parallel

Don't wait 90 days then launch. Capture content while building the platform.

```
Days 1-90:
  - Run sessions with Mask (capture)
  - Build course platform (prepare)
  - Build cohort enrollment system (prepare)
  - Drip free content to social media (warm audience)
  - Collect earning testimonials from Hub students (proof)

Day 90:
  - Launch first paid cohort with everything ready
```

Each session shapes the course. By Day 90 we have validated content, not theoretical content.

---

## Sequencing logic — why it works in this order

1. **Mask first** — Mask is the delivery mechanism for everything else. Without Mask, sessions don't scale, content doesn't multiply, and the whole story doesn't hold.

2. **Curriculum second** — Designing the syllabus before building course platform means the platform features map exactly to what we teach.

3. **Academy platform third** — With curriculum + Mask delivering, the platform is mostly UI for enrollment, payments, and progress.

4. **Mask licensing last** — Only sell what's proven. GRD must show real outcomes (students earning, students learning, sessions clipping into viral content) before pitching to other campuses.
